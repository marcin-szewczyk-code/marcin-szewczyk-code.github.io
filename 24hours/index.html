24-hour field recordings following the natural rhythm of places.

Not for active listening, but for presence.

About the Project

This project is meant to be experienced as something different from active listening.

Each recording lasts 24 hours and follows the natural rhythm of a place.

It is not a composition. Nothing is shortened or rearranged.

The recordings are meant to be present rather than listened to. They can be left playing in the background, gradually revealing change over time.

Some parts may seem uneventful at first, but can become meaningful with time.

First Recordings

The first recordings will be released in 2026.

The project begins with a first series recorded across different natural environments in Poland.
