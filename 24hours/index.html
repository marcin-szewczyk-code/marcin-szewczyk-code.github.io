24 Hours of Wild Poland

24-hour field recordings following the natural rhythm of places.

Not for active listening, but for presence.

About the Project

This is a project of 24-hour field recordings in a natural environment in Poland.

The recordings are divided into hourly segments, starting at 12:00 and ending at 12:00 the next day.

It is not a composition. Nothing is shortened or rearranged.

They can be left playing in the background, revealing change over time. You can start at any point, or at the corresponding hour to match real time.

Some parts may seem uneventful at first, but may become meaningful with time.

Listening

The recordings will be published on music distribution platforms and on a YouTube channel.

The first recordings will be released in 2026.
