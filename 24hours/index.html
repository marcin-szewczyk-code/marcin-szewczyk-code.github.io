About the Project

This project is meant to be experienced as something different from active listening.

Each recording lasts 24 hours and follows the natural rhythm of a place. It is not a composition. Nothing is shortened or rearranged.

These recordings are meant to be present rather than listened to. They can be left playing in the background, gradually revealing change over time.

What may seem uneventful at first may become meaningful with time.
